FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ESOP Guardian and ESOP management. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Feel free to reach out to our team.

What is an ESOP and how does it work?

An Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) allows employees to gain ownership in the company through stock options. After a vesting period, employees can buy shares at a set price, aligning their interests with the company's growth and aiding in talent retention.

How quickly can I set up an ESOP scheme with ESOP Guardian?

Our streamlined platform is designed for fast and seamless onboarding. Depending on the complexity of your company's structure, setup can typically be completed within a few days to a week, much faster than traditional methods.

Is ESOP Guardian compliant with Indian regulations?

Yes, absolutely. ESOP Guardian is meticulously designed to comply with the Companies Act, 2013, and all relevant SEBI/RBI guidelines for ESOPs in India. We provide automated compliance checks and reporting to keep you worry-free.

What is the cost of using ESOP Guardian?

Our pricing is based on your company's stage and number of employees. We offer transparent, tiered pricing models designed to scale with your growth, eliminating the high costs and hidden fees associated with manual management.

Can employees track their ESOPs on the platform?

Yes, we provide a dedicated employee portal where team members can view their grants, vesting schedules, exercise details, and track the real-time value of their equity, fostering trust and transparency.
